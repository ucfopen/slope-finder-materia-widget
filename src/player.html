Getting Started

Click on the blue coordinate points to move the line.

Select different "Display Options" to give additional information about the slope of the line.

Keyboard Controls

Use the Tab key
to select display options or points.
Use the Arrow keys
to move the points left, right, up, or down.
Press the "T" Key
to play/stop the graph's tone.
Press the "S" Key
to read out the slope.
Press the "H" Key
to Open or Close these instructions.
